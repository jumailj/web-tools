GuidLines
Normal font size between 16px and 32px "normal text" for Long text (like a blog post) size of 20px or even bigger
For headinlines you 50px+ and bold of 600+
Don't use a font weight under 400
Normal-size text use a line height between 1.5 and 2 . For big text go below 1.5
Decreases letter spacing in headlines, if it looks unnatural
Uppercase for short titles. make them small, bold and decrease letterspacing
Don't justify text
Don't center long text blocks. small blocks are fine.
